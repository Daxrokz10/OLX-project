Our Leader

Alec Oxenford co-founder

Oxenford is an art collector, focusing on Argentinian artists, such as Liliana Porter, Eduardo Navarro, Carlos Huffmann and Martin Legon. His collection includes about 280 pieces. In 2013, he became the president of the arteBA Foundation, the nonprofit that organizes the arteBA art fair, a Latin  American event. He previously worked for the Boston Consulting Group, and was the CEO of DeRemate.com, an online trading platform in Latin America, which sold to eBay.
